Projects I've Worked On:

150 Faces of Durham Virtual Exhibit

I developed and implemented the first version of a virtual exhibit featuring 150 historical figures of Durham for the Museum of Durham history. I was involved in all aspects of the development process, from initial designs to coding the front- and back-end to launching a production site, and will be continuing this project throughout the fall.

Wearable Microbial Air Collector

I designed, built, and tested a wearable working prototype device that would take in samples of airborne microbes from the surrounding environment. A research professor at Duke's Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering commissioned this project, as existing collection devices were expensive and too bulky to wear daily.
